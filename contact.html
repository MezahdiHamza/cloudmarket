Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping automatically.
What is your return policy? +
We offer 60-day hassle-free returns. Simply initiate a return from your order history, ship the item back in its original packaging, and we'll issue a full refund within 3–5 business days.
Are the products authentic? +
Absolutely. Every product on CloudMarket is 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We reject counterfeits at every step of our vetting process.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes! We ship to 120+ countries worldwide. International shipping times and rates vary by destination. All customs duties and import taxes are displayed transparently at checkout.
