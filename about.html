✦ Who We Are

Shopping Reimagined for the Modern Era

CloudMarket was founded in 2019 by a group of technologists and retail veterans who believed the online shopping experience was broken. Too many choices, too little trust, and no real curation — we set out to fix all three.

We spent two years building our product vetting process, assembling a world-class logistics network, and designing an experience that puts the customer first. Today, more than 50,000 customers trust CloudMarket as their go-to shopping destination.

From our headquarters in San Francisco, our team of 85 curates every single product on our platform. We don't list anything we wouldn't buy ourselves. That's our promise — and it's not negotiable.
